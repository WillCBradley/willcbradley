Philosophy

They focused on single projects for decades, and reaped the rewards of compound interest in the process.

But the world is speeding up.

The only constant factors are core principles, and these are too general to package and sell to the market on their own.

The alternative?

Rely on two other compounding vehicles: relationships and track record.
