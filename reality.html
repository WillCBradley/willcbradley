Reality

The world is made of people.

For millennia, our ancestors have pushed the frontier of technology audaciously towards the future.

But despite all these particle accelerators and antibiotics, us humans have remained the centre of attention.

This is not a coincidence.

Compared to other species (or computers) on earth, we’re uniquely capable of producing these incredible feats of knowledge.

Why does this matter?

Because while the world is made of humans, humans are bounded by human nature.

It can be uncomfortable to think of ourselves as transactional, predictable, brainstem-driven creatures…

But if we want to see the world clearly, we first need to see people clearly
